Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is URL encoding?
URL encoding converts special characters into a format that can be transmitted over the internet. Spaces become %20, and other special characters get their percent-encoded equivalents.
❓ When should I use URL encoding?
Use URL encoding when URLs contain spaces, non-ASCII characters (like Hindi text), or special characters like &, ?, # that could break the URL structure.
❓ What makes a URL SEO-friendly?
SEO-friendly URLs use hyphens instead of spaces, are lowercase, contain relevant keywords, and avoid special characters and unnecessary parameters.
