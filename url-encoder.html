Frequently Asked Questions (2026)

❓ What is URL encoding and why do I need it?
URL encoding (percent-encoding) converts special characters into a format safe for internet transmission. Spaces become %20, & becomes %26, and non-ASCII characters get encoded.
❓ What's the difference between encode and decode?
Encode converts special characters to percent-encoding (e.g., space → %20). Decode reverses this process (e.g., %20 → space).
