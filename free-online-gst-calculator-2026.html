What is the GST Model? A Global Case Study

GST is a multi-stage, destination-based tax applied on every value addition. India's model is divided into three components that explain tax division perfectly:

  • CGST (Central GST): Collected by the federal government on intra-state sales. Like the federal portion of Canada's HST.
  • SGST (State GST): Collected by the state/province government. Similar to state sales tax in the US.
  • IGST (Integrated GST): Applied on inter-state and international transactions. This is the model for cross-border taxation.
