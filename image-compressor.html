❓ Frequently Asked Questions (2026)

How does image compression work without losing quality?
Our tool uses smart lossy compression that removes invisible metadata, optimizes color data, and reduces precision in areas where the human eye can't detect differences. At 80% quality, visible differences are negligible while file size reduces 40-70% for JPEG and 25-50% for PNG. This directly improves your website's Core Web Vitals and SEO rankings.
What is WebP and why should I use it?
WebP is a modern image format by Google that provides 25-35% smaller files than JPEG/PNG at equivalent quality. It supports transparency (like PNG) and animation. Using WebP can significantly improve page load speed, Core Web Vitals scores, and overall SEO. All modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari 14+) support WebP.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No! Absolutely not. All compression happens locally in your browser. Your images NEVER leave your device. This ensures complete privacy and makes our tool safe for sensitive business images, personal photos, and confidential content. No data is collected, stored, or transmitted.
