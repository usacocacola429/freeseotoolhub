Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is the H1-H6 Heading Checker?
This tool analyzes the heading structure (H1, H2, H3, H4, H5, H6) of any webpage. It shows total headings, identifies missing or multiple H1 tags, and verifies proper heading hierarchy for 2026 SEO standards.
❓ How does the Headline Analyzer work in 2026?
The analyzer scores your headline based on word count, character length, power words, keyword presence, and emotional value. It provides a Google SERP preview and actionable suggestions updated for 2026 algorithms.
❓ What is the correct heading structure for SEO in 2026?
Each page should have exactly one H1 tag containing your primary keyword. Use H2 for main subheadings, H3 under H2, and maintain this hierarchy. This structure helps Google understand your content and improves rankings.
❓ What's the ideal headline length for 2026?
The ideal headline length is 50-70 characters (including spaces) with 6-12 words. This ensures your title displays fully in search results and social media shares.
