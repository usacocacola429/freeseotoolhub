Frequently Asked Questions

What is market saturation?

Market saturation measures how crowded a market or keyword is. High saturation means many competitors.

How is the score calculated?

Our algorithm analyzes global search results count, competitor density, and market indicators (1-100).

Can I check multiple markets?

Yes! Use bulk analysis to check up to 50 keywords or industries simultaneously.

Is this tool free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no registration, no limits.

What is a good opportunity rating?

Opportunity rating is inverse of saturation. 70%+ indicates excellent entry opportunities.

How often is data updated?

Data is fetched in real-time for accurate, up-to-date saturation analysis.
