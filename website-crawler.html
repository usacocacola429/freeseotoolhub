Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ Is this a free Screaming Frog alternative?
Yes! Our Website Crawler is a completely free alternative to Screaming Frog, allowing you to crawl up to 500 pages and identify technical SEO issues at no cost. It's ideal for small to medium websites and provides comprehensive SEO audit reports.
❓ How many pages can I crawl?
The free version allows crawling up to 500 pages per website. You can choose between 100, 250, or 500 page limits. This range is sufficient for most small to medium-sized websites for thorough SEO auditing.
❓ What SEO issues does the crawler detect?
The crawler detects broken links (404 errors), missing meta descriptions, duplicate title tags, missing H1 headings, images without alt text, slow-loading pages, redirect chains, schema markup issues, and other technical SEO problems.
❓ Is this tool updated for 2026 SEO standards?
Yes, the crawler is fully updated for 2026 SEO best practices including Core Web Vitals checks, mobile-first indexing analysis, E-E-A-T signals, and modern search engine requirements.
