What to Look For (Good Signs)

  • High Demand: 500+ monthly sales
  • Low Competition: Top products under 1000 reviews
  • Good Margins: 30%+ profit after FBA fees
  • Year-Round Demand: Not seasonal

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Too Competitive: Dominated by big brands
  • Low Margins: Under 20% profit
  • Seasonal Only: Sales drop significantly off-season
  • Legal/IP Issues: Trademark or patent risks
