Important Sitemap Information for SEO

Essential for Google: XML sitemaps help Google discover and index all pages of your website efficiently.

Submit to Google Search Console: After generating your sitemap, submit it through Google Search Console for faster indexing.
Keep It Updated: Update your sitemap whenever you add new pages and notify Google of changes.
Robots.txt Integration: Add your sitemap location to your robots.txt file for better search engine discovery.
