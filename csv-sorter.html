❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a CSV file?
CSV (Comma-Separated Values) is a simple file format used to store tabular data. Each line is a data record, and each record consists of fields separated by commas.
How to sort a CSV file?
Upload your CSV file, select the column you want to sort by, choose ascending or descending order, and click Process CSV.
Can I convert CSV to JSON?
Yes! Our tool allows you to convert CSV data to JSON, Excel, XML, and modified CSV formats with one click.
