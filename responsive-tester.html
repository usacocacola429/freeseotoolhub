Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is a responsive website tester?
A responsive website tester lets you preview how your website looks on different screen sizes including mobile, tablet, and desktop. It helps identify layout issues before going live.
❓ Why is responsive design important in 2026?
In 2026, over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. Google uses mobile-first indexing, making responsive design essential for SEO rankings.
❓ What screen sizes should I test?
Test at 375px (mobile), 768px (tablet), 1024px (laptop), and 1280px+ (desktop). Also test custom breakpoints relevant to your audience.
