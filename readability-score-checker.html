🟢 Easy Read (Grade 1-6)

  • General audience
  • High engagement
  • SEO friendly
  • Short sentences

🟡 Medium Read (Grade 7-12)

  • Business readers
  • Balanced complexity
  • Common for blogs
  • Moderate vocab

🔴 Hard Read (Grade 13+)

  • Expert audience
  • Technical content
  • Lower engagement
  • Long sentences
