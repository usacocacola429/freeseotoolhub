Common Mobile Issues

  • Slow Page Speed: Mobile networks are slower than desktop
  • Poor Touch Targets: Buttons too small for fingers
  • Intrusive Interstitials: Popups that cover content

Why do mobile and desktop rankings differ?

Improve mobile speed, use responsive design, optimize touch targets, and avoid intrusive popups.
