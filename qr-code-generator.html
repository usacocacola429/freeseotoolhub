Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is a QR code and how does it work?
A QR code is a two-dimensional barcode that stores information. When scanned with a smartphone camera, it instantly directs users to websites, WiFi networks, contact cards, and more.
❓ Can I add a logo to my QR code?
Yes! Upload your business logo and it will appear in the center of the QR code while maintaining scannability.
❓ What types of QR codes can I create?
You can create 8 types: URL links, plain text, WiFi credentials, business cards (vCard), email, phone numbers, SMS messages, and GPS locations.
