Frequently Asked Questions (2026)

❓ What is a QR code and how does it work?
A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that stores information readable by smartphone cameras. It can instantly direct users to websites, connect to WiFi, save contacts, send emails, and more. In 2026, QR codes are essential for contactless interactions, digital menus, payment systems, and marketing campaigns worldwide.
❓ Can I add my company logo to the QR code?
Yes! Our generator supports logo upload for branded QR codes. Upload your logo (PNG/JPG, max 1MB) and it appears in the center. The tool uses high error correction (Level H) to maintain scannability even with a logo in the center. A white border is automatically added around the logo for better contrast.
❓ What types of QR codes can I create?
You can create 8 types: URL (link to websites), Text (display any message), WiFi (share network credentials), vCard (digital business cards), Email (compose with subject/body), Phone (dial a number), SMS (send pre-written message), and Location (GPS coordinates).

Our QR codes generate at 250×250 pixels. For best print results, ensure the printed QR code is at least 2×2 cm (0.8×0.8 inches). The PNG format scales well for larger sizes. For business cards, we recommend printing at 1.5-2 cm square.
