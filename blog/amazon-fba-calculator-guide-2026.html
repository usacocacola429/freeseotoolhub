What is Amazon FBA?

Amazon FBA lets you store your products in Amazon's warehouses. Amazon handles storage, packing, shipping, returns, and customer service. In exchange, you pay FBA fees.

Amazon FBA Fees Explained

  • Referral Fee: 8-15% of sale price (varies by category)
  • Fulfillment Fee: Based on item size and weight
  • Storage Fee: Monthly fee for storing inventory
  • Closing Fee: Fixed fee per item sold
