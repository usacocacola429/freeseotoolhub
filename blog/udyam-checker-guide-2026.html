What is Udyam Registration?

Udyam Registration (earlier known as Udyog Aadhaar) is a government registration for MSMEs. It provides a unique Udyam Registration Number (URN) and an e-certificate. Registration is free, paperless, and based on self-declaration.

Benefits of Udyam Registration

  • Easy Loans: Collateral-free loans under CGTMSE scheme
  • Government Tenders: Preference in government procurement
  • Tax Benefits: Various state and central tax exemptions
  • Subsidies: Subsidy on patent registration, ISO certification
  • Electricity Discounts: Concessional electricity rates in some states

Udyam Registration Ke Liye Kaun Eligible Hai?

  • Micro Enterprise: Investment up to ₹1 crore, turnover up to ₹5 crore
  • Small Enterprise: Investment up to ₹10 crore, turnover up to ₹50 crore
  • Medium Enterprise: Investment up to ₹50 crore, turnover up to ₹250 crore
