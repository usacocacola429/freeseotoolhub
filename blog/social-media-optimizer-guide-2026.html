Why Social Media Optimization Matters

Social media algorithms (Facebook EdgeRank, Instagram Algorithm, Twitter/X Algorithm) prioritize content that gets engagement. Optimization directly impacts visibility. Key factors include:

  • Post Length: Each platform has optimal character limits
  • Hashtags: Right hashtags increase discoverability
  • Media: Posts with images/videos get 2x more engagement
  • Timing: Posting when your audience is active

Social Media Post Optimization by Platform

Facebook

  • Optimal post length: 40-80 characters
  • Link posts outperform photo-only posts
  • Best time: 9 AM - 1 PM local time

Instagram

  • Caption length: 138-150 characters
  • 5-10 relevant hashtags (mix of popular and niche)
  • Carousel posts get highest engagement

Twitter/X

  • Tweet length: 71-100 characters for highest engagement
  • 1-2 hashtags maximum
  • Include images or GIFs when possible
