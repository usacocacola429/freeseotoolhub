How Does Our AI Detector Work?

Our AI content detector uses advanced machine learning models trained on millions of human-written and AI-generated texts. It analyzes:

  • Perplexity: How predictable the text is. AI text tends to be more predictable.
  • Burstiness: Variation in sentence structure. AI text tends to be more uniform.
  • Word Repetition: AI models often repeat certain words and phrases.
  • Sentence Length: AI text often has consistent sentence lengths.
  • Vocabulary Richness: Human text tends to have a richer vocabulary.

Based on these metrics, the tool gives you a confidence score indicating the likelihood that the text was AI-generated.
