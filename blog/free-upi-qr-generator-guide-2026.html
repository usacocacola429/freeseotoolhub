What is UPI? A World-Leading Payment System

UPI is a system that powers multiple bank accounts into a single mobile application, merging several banking features, seamless fund routing & merchant payments into one hood. It allows any user to send or receive money instantly using a simple virtual payment address (VPA), like `yourname@bank`. This eliminates the need for typing lengthy bank account numbers or IFSC codes.
