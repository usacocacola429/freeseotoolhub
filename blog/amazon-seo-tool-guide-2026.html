What is Amazon SEO and Why Does It Matter?

Amazon SEO is the process of optimizing your product listings to rank higher in Amazon's search results. Unlike Google SEO, Amazon's A10 algorithm focuses heavily on sales performance, conversion rates, and relevance. Higher rankings = more visibility = more sales.

How Amazon's A10 Algorithm Works in 2026

Amazon's A10 algorithm ranks products based on:

  • Relevance: How well your title, bullets, and description match the search query
  • Sales Velocity: How many units you're selling consistently
  • Conversion Rate: What percentage of visitors buy your product
  • Backend Keywords: Hidden search terms that help Amazon understand your product
  • Reviews & Ratings: Social proof heavily impacts rankings

Amazon SEO Best Practices for 2026

1. Optimize Product Titles

Your title is the most important element. Include main keyword, brand, size, color, and key features. Amazon allows 200 characters.

2. Use Backend Search Terms

Amazon gives you 250 bytes of backend search terms. Use synonyms, alternate spellings, and related keywords that don't fit in your title or bullets.

3. Write Benefit-Focused Bullet Points

Focus on benefits, not just features. Explain how the product solves a problem.
