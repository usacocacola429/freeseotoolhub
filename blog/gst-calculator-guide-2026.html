GST Kya Hai? (What is GST?)

GST ek comprehensive indirect tax hai jo pure India mein lagu hai. Isne VAT, Service Tax, Excise Duty jaise kai taxes ko replace kar diya hai.

GST Ke Types

  • CGST (Central GST): Central government ko jata hai
  • SGST (State GST): State government ko jata hai
  • IGST (Integrated GST): Inter-state transactions ke liye

GST Slabs in India 2026

  • 0%: Essential items like fresh vegetables, milk, eggs
  • 5%: Packaged food items, tea, coffee
  • 12%: Mobile phones, processed food
  • 18%: Most services, electronics
  • 28%: Luxury items, cars, tobacco
