What Are Broken Links?

A broken link (also called dead link) is a hyperlink on your website that points to a page that no longer exists. When users click it, they see a "404 Not Found" error. Common causes include:

  • Deleting a page without setting up redirects
  • Changing a page URL without updating internal links
  • Linking to external websites that have removed their content
  • Typing errors in URLs when creating links

Why Broken Links Hurt Your SEO

  1. Wasted Crawl Budget: Googlebot spends time crawling dead pages instead of your important content
  2. Poor User Experience: Users who hit 404 errors are likely to leave your site
  3. Link Equity Loss: Backlinks pointing to 404 pages lose their SEO value
  4. Lower Quality Signals: Too many errors signal poor website maintenance to Google
