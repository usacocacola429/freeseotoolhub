Why are Internal Links Important for SEO?

  • Helps Google Understand Your Site Structure: Internal links show Google how your pages are related and which ones are most important.
  • Distributes Page Authority (Link Juice): Links from high-authority pages pass authority to other pages, boosting their rankings.
  • Improves User Experience: Internal links guide users to relevant content, keeping them on your site longer.
  • Reduces Bounce Rate: When users find more relevant content, they stay longer and explore more pages.
  • Helps Indexing: Internal links help Google discover and index new pages faster.
  • Increases Pageviews: Good internal linking increases the number of pages a user visits per session.

Internal Linking Best Practices in 2026

1. Use Descriptive Anchor Text

Always use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text. Avoid generic phrases like "click here" or "read more".

  • Bad: Click here to learn about SEO.
  • Good: Learn more about SEO best practices for 2026.

2. Link to High-Value Pages

Link to your most important pages (pillar posts, cornerstone content) from multiple pages to pass authority.

3. Keep a Flat Site Structure

Ensure that every page on your site is reachable within 3 clicks from the homepage.

4. Use Relevant Context

Only add internal links when they are relevant to the context. Don't force links where they don't belong.

5. Link from New Pages to Old Pages

When you publish new content, always link back to your older, high-authority pages.

6. Use a Variety of Links

Don't always link to the same page. Use a variety of internal links to distribute authority evenly.

7. Regularly Audit Your Links

Check your internal links periodically to ensure they are not broken or pointing to outdated content.

Internal Links vs. External Links

Feature Internal Links External Links
Definition Links from one page on your site to another page on your site. Links from your site to other websites.
SEO Purpose Distribute authority, improve site structure, help indexing. Build credibility, provide references, earn backlinks.
Authority Passes authority within your own site. Passes authority to external sites (and loses some).
Control You have full control over anchor text and placement. You cannot control how others link to you.
Best Practice Link to your most important pages regularly. Link to authoritative, relevant external sources.
