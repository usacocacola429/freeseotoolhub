Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is the Competitor SERP Analyzer?
This free tool analyzes the top 10 Google search results for any keyword, showing Domain Authority, Page Authority, backlinks, content length, and keyword usage to help you understand competition.
❓ What metrics does this tool analyze?
The tool analyzes Domain Authority (DA), Page Authority (PA), backlinks, referring domains, content length, title optimization, keyword density, and provides a difficulty score.
❓ How is the Difficulty Score calculated?
The score combines DA (40%), backlinks (30%), and content length (30%) to estimate ranking difficulty. Higher scores indicate stronger competition.
❓ What is a good Domain Authority in 2026?
DA ranges from 1-100. Scores above 60 are considered strong, 40-60 is average, and below 40 may be easier to compete against.

Understanding SEO Metrics

What is DA?

Domain Authority predicts how well a website will rank on search engines. Higher DA (80+) means stronger ranking potential.

Why check backlinks?

Backlinks are votes of confidence. More quality backlinks = higher trust and better rankings.

Content length?

Top ranking pages typically have 1500-2500+ words. Quality content ranks higher.

Difficulty Score

Combined metric based on DA, backlinks, and content quality to show competition level.
