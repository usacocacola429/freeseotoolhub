Frequently Asked Questions

What is Domain Authority?

Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how likely a website is to rank in search engine result pages (SERPs). It ranges from 1-100, with higher scores indicating greater ranking ability.

Why analyze backlinks?

Backlinks are crucial for SEO as they act as "votes of confidence" from other websites. Analyzing competitor backlinks helps you understand their link building strategies and identify opportunities for your own website.

What is ideal content length?

While there's no perfect length, top-ranking pages typically have comprehensive content (1500-2500+ words). However, content quality and relevance are more important than word count alone.

How to use this data?

Use competitor analysis to identify content gaps, understand what makes top pages rank, and develop a data-driven SEO strategy. Focus on creating better, more comprehensive content than your competitors.
