SSL Certificate Best Practices for 2026

HTTPS is Essential: Google uses HTTPS as a ranking signal. Chrome marks non-HTTPS sites as "Not Secure". Always use SSL.
Auto-Renewal: Enable auto-renewal through your hosting provider or CDN (Cloudflare). Let's Encrypt offers free 90-day certificates.
TLS 1.3 Preferred: Upgrade to TLS 1.3 for faster handshakes and stronger security. Remove support for TLS 1.0/1.1 (deprecated).
Monitor Expiry: Check SSL monthly. Set calendar reminders 30, 14, and 7 days before expiry to avoid unexpected downtime.
