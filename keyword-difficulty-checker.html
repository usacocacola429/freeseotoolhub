Benefits of Keyword Difficulty Analysis

  • Save Time: Focus on keywords you can actually rank for
  • Better ROI: Target low-competition, high-volume keywords
  • Strategic Planning: Build content around achievable goals
  • Competitor Insights: Understand market competition level

Common Keyword Research Mistakes

  • Targeting Only High-Volume Keywords: Often too competitive for new sites
  • Ignoring Search Intent: Difficulty score doesn't measure intent match
  • Not Checking Regularly: Competition changes over time
  • Relying on Single Metric: Combine with volume and CPC analysis

Keyword Difficulty Guide

🟢 Easy (1-30)

  • Long-tail phrases
  • Low competition
  • Quick ranking
  • Ideal for new sites

🟡 Medium (31-70)

  • Balanced competition
  • Moderate volume
  • Quality content needed
  • Build authority

🔴 Hard (71-100)

  • High-volume terms
  • Strong authority needed

Frequently Asked Questions

What is keyword difficulty?

A score from 1-100 indicating how hard it is to rank for a keyword. Lower scores are easier.

How is difficulty calculated?

Analyzes Google results, competing pages, and domain authority of top-ranking sites.

Can I check multiple keywords?

Yes! Use bulk mode to analyze up to 50 keywords at once.

Is this tool free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no limits.

What's a good difficulty score?

New sites: below 30. Established: 31-70. High authority: 71-100.

How often should I check?

Check monthly as competition and search trends change.
