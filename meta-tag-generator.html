What are meta tags?

Titles: 50-60 characters. Descriptions: 150-160 characters. Our tool has real-time character counting.

What are Open Graph tags?

They control how your content appears when shared on Facebook, LinkedIn, and other platforms.

How to implement these tags?

Copy the HTML code and paste it into the <head> section of your webpage, before </head>.

What is JSON-LD?
