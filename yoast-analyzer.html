Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is Yoast SEO analysis?
Yoast SEO analysis evaluates content against SEO best practices including keyword usage, readability, meta descriptions, internal/external links, and content structure to help improve search rankings.
❓ What is a good Yoast SEO score?
A score of 80+ (green) is considered excellent. 60-79 (orange) needs improvement. Below 60 (red) requires significant optimization work.
❓ What factors does this analyzer check?
The analyzer checks keyword in title/first paragraph, keyword density, content length, meta description length, readability, outbound links, internal links, and image alt text.
