Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is Udyam Registration?
Udyam Registration is the government MSME registration for micro, small, and medium enterprises in India. It provides a unique 16-digit Udyam Registration Number.
❓ How to check Udyam Registration status?
Enter your Udyam Number, PAN, Aadhar, or Application Number in the checker above. For official verification, visit udyamregistration.gov.in.
