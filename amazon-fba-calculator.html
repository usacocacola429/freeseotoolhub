FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) Pros

  • Prime Eligibility: Products get Prime badge, increasing conversion by 30%+
  • Hands-off Fulfillment: Amazon handles storage, packing, shipping, and returns
  • Customer Service: Amazon handles customer inquiries and returns 24/7
  • Buy Box Advantage: FBA sellers win Buy Box more often
  • Multi-channel Fulfillment: Use FBA for orders from other platforms

MFN (Merchant Fulfilled) Pros

  • Lower Fees: No FBA fulfillment or storage fees
  • Inventory Control: You control your inventory and can bundle products easily
  • No Long-term Storage Fees: Avoid penalties for slow-moving inventory
  • Better for Oversized Items: Large/heavy items are cheaper to fulfill yourself
  • Custom Packaging: Add branded inserts and custom packaging

Frequently Asked Questions

FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon) is a service where Amazon stores your products, picks, packs, and ships them to customers, and handles customer service and returns. This allows sellers to focus on sourcing products while Amazon handles logistics.

FBA fees include: Referral fee (8-20% of selling price by category), Fulfillment fee (based on size/weight), Monthly storage fee ($0.83 per cubic foot Jan-Sep, $2.40 Oct-Dec), and additional fees for returns, disposal, or long-term storage (365+ days).

FBA (Fulfillment by Amazon): Amazon handles storage, shipping, and customer service. Products are eligible for Prime. Higher fees but less work.
MFN (Merchant Fulfilled Network): You handle storage, shipping, and customer service. Lower fees but requires time and effort for fulfillment.

A good ROI for Amazon FBA is typically 50-100%. This means for every $1 invested, you make $1.50-$2.00 in revenue. After fees, aim for at least 20-30% net profit margin.

Our calculator uses Amazon's official 2026 fee structure and provides accurate estimates. Fees are updated regularly. However, actual fees may vary slightly based on category nuances, seasonal changes, and specific product dimensions. Always verify with Amazon's official FBA calculator.
