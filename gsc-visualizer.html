Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ How do I export data from Google Search Console?
Go to Google Search Console > Performance report. Click the Export button and choose CSV format. Upload that file to our visualizer.
❓ What metrics can I visualize?
You can visualize clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position. Filter by date range, compare periods, and view data by queries, pages, countries, and devices.
❓ Is this tool free?
Yes, the GSC Data Visualizer is completely free on FreeSEOToolHub.com. You can also load sample data to explore features.
