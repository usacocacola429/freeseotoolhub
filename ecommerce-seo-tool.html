Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Amazon and Flipkart SEO?

Amazon emphasizes backend keywords and A+ Content for better visibility, while Flipkart prioritizes complete specification tables and visual content. Amazon allows up to 250 characters of hidden keywords, whereas Flipkart focuses more on detailed product attributes and specifications.

Is this e-commerce SEO tool completely free?

Yes! This tool is 100% free with no registration, signup, or usage limits. You can optimize unlimited product listings for both Amazon and Flipkart platforms without any restrictions.

How can I improve my SEO score?

To improve your SEO score: keep titles between 50-200 characters, write descriptions of 500+ characters, use 5-15 relevant keywords, select the correct category, and include pricing information. Higher scores indicate better-optimized listings.

Which platform should I prioritize - Amazon or Flipkart?

Both platforms have unique advantages. Amazon typically has higher traffic and international reach, while Flipkart may offer lower competition in certain market segments. Our tool optimizes for both, allowing you to maximize visibility everywhere.
