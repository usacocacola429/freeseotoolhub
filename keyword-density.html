Frequently Asked Questions - 2026

❓ What is the ideal keyword density for SEO in 2026?
For English content, ideal density is 0.5-2.5%. For Hindi content, 1.5-2.5% works best. Focus on natural placement and user intent rather than exact percentages.
❓ Does this tool support Hindi content?
Yes! Our tool fully supports Hindi (Devanagari) content analysis with accurate word counting and density calculations.
❓ What is keyword stuffing?
Keyword stuffing is overusing keywords unnaturally. Google penalizes this practice. Keep density under 3% and use synonyms naturally.
