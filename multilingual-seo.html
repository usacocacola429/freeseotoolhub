 Multilingual SEO FAQ

        

          
Q: What are hreflang tags?

          
Hreflang tags are HTML attributes that tell search engines which language and regional variations of a page exist. They help prevent duplicate content issues and ensure users see the correct language version.

        

        

          
Q: What is the Bhashini API?

          
Bhashini API is India's national language translation platform supporting 22+ Indian languages with machine translation, speech recognition, and transliteration services.

 Multilingual SEO Tips 2026
