What is Hreflang?

Hreflang is an HTML attribute that tells Google which language and regional URL to show in search results. Essential for multilingual websites to avoid duplicate content and show the right content to the right users.

  • ✅ Prevents duplicate content penalties
  • ✅ Shows right language to right users
  • ✅ Improves international SEO
  • ✅ Increases conversion rates
