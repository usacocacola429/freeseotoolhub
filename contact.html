Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions here.

How can I suggest a new tool? +

You can suggest a new tool by:

  • Using the form above and selecting "New Tool Suggestion"
  • Emailing us directly at: suggestions@freeseotoolhub.com
  • Providing detailed info: the tool's purpose, key features, and target audience

We carefully review all suggestions and prioritize the most popular ones.

What should I do if I find a bug? +

If you find a bug or issue with a tool, please:

  1. Take a screenshot or video recording of the issue
  2. Note your browser name and version
  3. Describe when and how the issue occurred
  4. Report it via the form or by emailing bugs@freeseotoolhub.com

We'll work to fix it as quickly as possible.

Will your tools always be free? +

Yes, all our core tools will remain free forever. This is the central part of our mission.

We fund the site through:

  • Google AdSense advertising
  • Optional premium features (in the future)
  • Sponsorships and collaborations

But the basic toolkit will never be locked behind a paywall.

How can I partner with FreeSEOToolHub? +

For partnerships, send your portfolio and proposal to contact@freeseotoolhub.com.
