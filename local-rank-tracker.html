Frequently Asked Questions

What is Local Rank Tracking?

Local rank tracking shows how your website ranks for specific keywords in different cities or regions. It's essential for businesses targeting local customers.

Why track rankings city-wise?

Search results vary by location. A keyword that ranks #1 in Mumbai might rank #15 in Delhi. City-wise tracking helps optimize local SEO strategy.

How often should I track local rankings?

Track weekly for active campaigns and monthly for stable rankings. Our tool updates data in real-time for accurate monitoring.

What's a good local rank?

Top 3 positions get 75% of clicks. Aim for positions 1-3 in your target cities for maximum visibility and traffic.
