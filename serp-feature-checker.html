Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Featured Snippet?

Featured Snippets are selected search results that appear at the top of Google's results in a box. They typically answer a question directly from a webpage.

What is People Also Ask?

PAA boxes show related questions that people frequently search for. Clicking a question expands to show an answer and link to the source.

How to get featured snippets?

Answer questions clearly and concisely in your content. Use headers (H2, H3) that match question phrases, and provide direct answers in 40-50 words.

Why check SERP features?

Understanding SERP features helps you optimize for zero-click searches, identify question-based keywords, and capture more organic traffic.
