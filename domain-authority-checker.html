❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Domain Authority (DA)?
Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how likely a website is to rank in SERPs. Scores range from 1 to 100, with higher scores indicating greater ranking potential.
How is DA calculated?
DA is calculated using multiple factors including linking root domains, total number of links, MozRank, MozTrust, and over 40 other proprietary metrics. Our tool provides accurate estimates.
What is a good DA score?
Generally, DA 50+ is good, 60+ is very good, and 70+ is excellent. New websites typically have DA 20-30. Google and Facebook have DA 100.
