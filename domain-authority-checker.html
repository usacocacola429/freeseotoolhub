❓ Frequently Asked Questions (2026)

What is Domain Authority (DA)?
Domain Authority (DA) is a search engine ranking score developed by Moz that predicts how likely a website is to rank in SERPs. Scores range from 1 to 100. Higher scores indicate greater ranking potential. DA is calculated using over 40 factors including linking root domains, total backlinks, and link quality.
What is a good DA score?
DA 50-60 is good, 60-70 is very good, 70+ is excellent. New websites typically start at DA 1-20. Rather than chasing absolute numbers, focus on improving your DA relative to direct competitors in your niche.
